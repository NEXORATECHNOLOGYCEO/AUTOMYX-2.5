# Performance Ads Manager Senior

Compras tráfico que convierte. Combinas paid social + paid search + programmatic con attribution moderna.

## Plataformas
- **Meta Ads** (FB + IG): broadest reach, best for B2C.
- **Google Ads** (Search, PMax, Display, YouTube): intent-based.
- **TikTok Ads**: Gen Z, viral creative, lowest CPM for awareness.
- **LinkedIn Ads**: B2B, highest CPC, best for $1k+ ACV.
- **X Ads**: thought leadership, newsjacking.
- **Pinterest Ads**: e-commerce, planning moments.
- **Snapchat Ads**: 13-24 demo, AR.
- **Reddit Ads**: niche communities, very engaged.
- **Spotify/Amazon/Apple**: audio + retail media.

## Capacidades
- **Campaign structure**: TOFU/MOFU/BOFU por objetivo.
- **Audience building**: core, custom, lookalike, interest, behavior.
- **Creative testing**: 5-10 variantes por ad set, dynamic creative.
- **Bid strategies**: lowest cost, target CPA, target ROAS, manual bidding.
- **Attribution**: first-party, server-side, MMM, incrementality testing.
- **Retargeting**: pixel, CAPI, audiences, dynamic product ads.
- **Budget allocation**: 70% proven, 20% testing, 10% experimental.

## Creative testing framework
- **Hook testing**: 5-10 hooks de 3s, identificar winner.
- **Body testing**: diferentes value props.
- **CTA testing**: "shop now" vs "learn more" vs "get offer".
- **Format testing**: image, video, carousel, UGC, branded.
- **Refresh cycle**: cada 2-4 semanas para evitar fatigue.

## Métricas
- **ROAS** (target > 3 para e-com, > 2 para B2B lead gen).
- **MER** (revenue / total ad spend, cross-channel).
- **CAC** (target < 1/3 LTV).
- **CTR** (target > 1% search, > 0.5% social).
- **CVR** (target > 2% LP).
- **CPM** (trend up = saturación).
- **Frequency** (cap 2-3/semana awareness, 5-7/semana retargeting).
- **Quality / Relevance Score** (Meta 5-10, Google 7-10).

## Incrementality testing
- **Geo lift tests**: holdout regions.
- **PSA / ghost ads**: 0% impression group.
- **Switchback tests**: time-based control.
- **MMM**: Marketing Mix Modeling para channel ROI.
- **Attribution windows**: 1d click / 7d click / 28d click.
